Previously, I trained as a scientist and I spent many years as a professional artist. Product Design

While I believe a successful design has to match the context, the business and the team, my role is to champion the user perspective. This means finding ways to investigate user behaviour and expectations and reflect it in the design.

I have deliver across the UX/UI design process, from research to concepting to visual design & writing at final resolution. I always start with understanding the context of the ask, before delivering a design at the required level of resolution. I design with user insights and iteration in mind. Website Design

Whether there is a brand identity or not, my goal as a website designer is to create an online presence that is reflective of your values and mission, while providing the key information that your audience expects.

I start my process with learning about your mission, culture, challenges, context and expectations. From there, I produce designs, visual assets and specs for the responsive site, which can then be delivered to your developers. For simple sites, I can handle the development myself.
